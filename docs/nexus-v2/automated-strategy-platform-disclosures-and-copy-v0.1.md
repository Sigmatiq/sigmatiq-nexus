# Automated Strategy Platform: Disclosures and Copy v0.1

**Status:** Draft supporting specification for legal/compliance review  
**Supports:** Automated Strategy Platform Requirements v1.3  
**Purpose:** Provide consistent user-facing language for paper trading, automation policies, strategy events, fills, risk, and multi-leg limitations.

---

## 1. Core Copy Rules

Use this language pattern:

```text
Strategy condition matched. Your policy will decide whether this becomes a paper trade.
```

Do not use:

```text
Recommended trade
Best contract
Guaranteed setup
You should buy
```

---

## 2. Strategy Event Copy

```text
A strategy event is a technical market condition generated by the system. It is not a personalized recommendation or instruction to trade.
```

If candidates are shown:

```text
Candidate contracts are shown for policy evaluation. Your enabled policy controls whether any paper order is created.
```

---

## 3. Paper Trading Copy

```text
Paper trading is simulated. Paper fills, prices, slippage, and exits may differ materially from live broker execution.
```

For performance screens:

```text
Paper performance is not live performance and does not guarantee future results.
```

---

## 4. Fill Realism Copy

```text
Fill realism controls how conservative the paper execution model is. A more realistic or conservative model may reduce simulated fills and lower simulated returns.
```

For REALISTIC:

```text
REALISTIC mode estimates fill probability using spread position, quote age, spread width, liquidity, order side, and time of day.
```

---

## 5. Stop-Loss Copy

```text
Option-premium stops can trigger because of underlying price movement, theta decay, implied-volatility changes, or spread movement.
```

For delayed option-premium stops:

```text
Your option-premium stop is not armed yet. Underlying-price stops and daily-loss controls remain active if configured.
```

---

## 6. Daily-Loss Circuit Breaker Copy

```text
Your daily-loss limit has been reached. New entries are blocked, open entry orders are cancelled, and open positions will be exited or marked pending exit when valid quotes are available.
```

If quote unavailable:

```text
Exit is pending because no valid quote is available. The system will re-evaluate when quotes resume.
```

---

## 7. Shadow Outcome Copy

```text
This is a hypothetical shadow outcome for a trade your policy rejected. It did not affect your paper P&L and was not an actual paper trade.
```

Policy tuning copy:

```text
Shadow outcomes are best reviewed in aggregate by rejection reason. Individual hypothetical winners should not be treated as missed trades.
```

---

## 8. Multi-Leg Paper Copy

```text
Multi-leg paper fills are modeled atomically. Live broker handling of combo orders may differ and can include rejection, partial fill, or legging risk.
```

For max loss:

```text
Displayed max loss is theoretical and assumes expiration settlement, no early assignment, no exercise, no corporate action, and valid contract multipliers.
```

---

## 9. Confidence Copy

Avoid displaying raw precision such as `0.7241`.

Preferred:

```text
Confidence bucket: Medium
```

Optional context:

```text
This event's confidence is in the top 30% of historical events for this strategy version.
```

---

## 10. Live Trading Future-Phase Copy

```text
Live trading is not enabled in this MVP. Any future live order drafting or live auto-execution requires separate legal, compliance, broker, operational, and user authorization gates.
```
